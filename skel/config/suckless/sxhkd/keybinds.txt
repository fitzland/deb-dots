DWM KEY BINDINGS
----------------
Super + Return --- Zoom
Super + q --- Close window
Super + p --- Launch dmenu
Super + Shift + Return --- terminal
Super + o --- Change Focus Right
Super + i --- Change Focus Left
Super + Ctrl + h --- Resize Left 
Super + Ctrl + l --- Resize Right
Super + Shift + n --- Toggle WindowFollow
Super + Shift + o --- Move Window Right
Super + Shift + i --- Move Window Left
Super + Shift + q --- Quit DWM
Super + Shift + r --- Restart DWM
Super + x --- Logout/Reboot/Poweroff

DWM TAG BINDINGS
----------------
Super + (1-9) --- Switch to tag (workspace 1-9)
Super + Shift + (1-9) ---  Move Window to tag (workspace 1-9)
Super + 0 --- Switch to tag 10
Super + minus --- Switch to tag 11
Super + equal --- Switch to tag 12
Super + Shift + 0 --- Move to tag 10
Super + Shift + minus --- Move to tag 11
Super + Shift + equal --- Move to tag 12
Super + Tab --- Last Tag
Ctrl  + Shift + Right --- View Tag to Right
Ctrl  + Shift + Left --- View Tag to Left
Ctrl  + Alt + Right --- Move Window to Right Tag
Ctrl  + Alt + Left --- Move Window to Left Tag
