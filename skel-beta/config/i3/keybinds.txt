i3 KEY BINDINGS
------------------
Super + Space --- Rofi Menu
Super + q --- Close window
Super + Right --- Change Focus Right
Super + Left --- Change Focus Left
Super + Shift + Right --- Move Window Right
Super + Shift + Left --- Move Window Left
Super + Ctrl + Left --- Resize Left 
Super + Ctrl + Right --- Resize Right
Super + Ctrl + Up --- Resize Up 
Super + Ctrl + Down --- Resize Down

Super + Shift + q --- Quit i3
Super + Shift + r --- Restart i3
Super + ESC --- Restart SXHKD
Super + h --- This screen

i3 TAG BINDINGS
----------------
Super + (1-9,0) --- Switch to tag (workspace 1-10)
Super + Shift + (1-9,0) ---  Move Window to tag (workspace 1-10)
	
SXHKD KEY BINDINGS
------------------
Super + Space --- Rofi Menu
Super + Return --- Tilix
Super + Shift + Return --- Kitty (floating)
Super + b --- Firefox-ESR
Super + Shift + b --- Firefox-ESR -private-window
Super + f --- Thunar
Super + e --- Geany
Super + v --- Pulsemixer

AUDIO
-----
Super + Insert --- Volume Up
Super + Delete --- Volume Down
Super + m --- Muted

SCROT
-----
Super + @Print --- Select Rectangle Screenshot
@Print --- Fullscreen Screenshot

REDSHIFT
--------
Super + Alt + r --- Redshift On (night)
Super + Alt + b --- Redshift off (daytime)
